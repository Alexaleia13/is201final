My Story

Music has been part of my life for as long as I can remember. The piano was my first love — there's something about sitting down at the keys and letting everything else disappear. Singing came naturally alongside it, and together they became my favorite way to express things words alone can't capture.

Alongside my accounting degree, I've always dreamed of writing and recording original music. Someday, I want to tell my own stories through song.

What I Love About Music

Music touches every part of life. Here's how I think about it:

  1. Playing Instruments
    • Piano — classical and contemporary styles
    • Learning chord progressions by ear
    • Experimenting with melodies I make up
  2. Singing
    • Vocal harmonies
    • Singing in Spanish from my mission experience
    • Matching emotion to melody
  3. Songwriting
    • Finding the right words for a feeling
    • Building a song from a single chord
    • Telling stories through lyrics

🎵 My Musical Influences

These are the artists and styles that shaped how I hear and feel music:

  • Yiruma — peaceful, emotional piano compositions
  • Adele — raw vocal storytelling
  • Coldplay — the blend of piano and atmosphere
  • Spanish folk music — from my time as a missionary
  • Classic hymns — where my love of harmony began
